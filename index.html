Who We Are

A company born from climbing.

Founded in 1973 by rock climber Yvon Chouinard, Patagonia designs and sells high-performance outdoor apparel, gear, and food. Our product lines span alpine climbing, skiing, snowboarding, surfing, fly fishing, mountain biking, and trail running — what we call the silent sports.

We are headquartered in Ventura, California and operate worldwide with retail stores across North America, Europe, South America, Asia, and Australia. Our Reno, Nevada distribution center supports all wholesale and direct-to-consumer operations.

In 2022, Chouinard transferred company ownership — valued at approximately $3 billion — to the Patagonia Purpose Trust and Holdfast Collective, a nonprofit. Every dollar not reinvested in the business now goes directly to fighting the environmental crisis.

Protecting places like this is why we exist.

Your Schedule

Work Hours & Flexibility

We operate on a 9/80 work schedule: nine hours Monday through Thursday, eight hours on alternating Fridays. That's a three-day weekend every other week — 26 long weekends a year.

Corporate hours are generally 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM. Retail varies by store and season. Distribution offers 15+ scheduling options with schedules guaranteed three weeks in advance.

Our famous "Let My People Go Surfing" flex-time policy: when conditions are right, head out — as long as the work gets done. This isn't a gimmick. It's how we've always operated.

Feedback & Recognition

Performance Appraisals

Performance is evaluated through annual goal setting and ongoing feedback. Reviews consider your impact, alignment with company values, collaboration, and growth.

All U.S. regular employees are bonus-eligible, with hourly employees starting at a 5% bonus. After six months, hourly retail, CX, and distribution employees receive a $0.50/hour progression raise.

Feedback here is a continuous, two-way conversation — we listen as much as we evaluate.

Voluntary Separation

Should you decide to move on, we respectfully ask that you provide at least two (2) weeks' written notice to your direct manager. This ensures a smooth transition for your team. We value the relationships built here and want your departure to be as positive as your time with us.

Culture Fit Matters

When we read resumes, we start from the bottom. Your hobbies and passions tell us who you are. We want people who live the outdoor life. It's not unusual for positions to stay open for a year — we wait for the exact right person.

Sustainability Is Everyone's Job

100% of our U.S. facilities run on renewable energy. 69% of raw materials come from recycled sources, and we're working to eliminate virgin petroleum-based materials entirely. Through our Worn Wear program, we encourage customers to repair and reuse gear.

Key Locations

Ventura, CA — Headquarters (~700 employees). Product design, marketing, HR, finance, and more.
Reno, NV — Distribution center (~850 team members). Shipping, repairs, CX leadership.
70+ retail stores worldwide across 6 continents.

Ironclad Guarantee

We guarantee everything we make. If a product doesn't perform as intended, we'll repair, replace, or refund it. Encourage every customer to trust this — it's real, and it's been our promise from day one.
